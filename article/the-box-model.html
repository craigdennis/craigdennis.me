The Box Model

You will find lots of articles about the Box Model online as it is a important concept to get your head around. The Box model is required to help make your content look pretty and not sqaushed on your page.

The concept to get into your head is that all the elements on a website are basically rectangle. From the buttons, the images and your text. This are all rectangles that are placed on your webpage.

Where the box model comes in, is how you can manipulate certain properties such as padding, margin, border, to change the size of the box in relation to the contents.

Let's look at the content first.

Content

            width: 100px;
